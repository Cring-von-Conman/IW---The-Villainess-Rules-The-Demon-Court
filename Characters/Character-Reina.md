#character

**Reina Steelhart** _The Devoted Knight. The Only Pure Thoughts in a Castle Full of Noise._

Childhood friend and sworn protector. Volunteered to follow Scarlet into demon territory — certain death for a human — without hesitation. Small, fierce, and absolutely deadly with a blade. The one person in the castle who knew her before.

---

## Appearance

Short green hair, perpetually a mess. Fierce amber eyes. Small build that is deeply misleading about her lethality. Currently: travel-stained training armour and a fresh bruise darkening on her jaw courtesy of Commander Vorlag. She broke his wrist in return.

---

## Nature

Human. Entirely, stubbornly, defiantly human in a castle full of demons, vampires, and at least one primordial force of nature. Has no magic, no special abilities, no political leverage. Has a sword, a spine, and a devotion that doesn't require any of those things.

Her thoughts are the only pure ones Scarlet has encountered since transmigration. No schemes, no hunger, no performance. Just love, protection, loyalty — exactly as they appear on the surface. In a castle where everyone's interior monologue is a tactical minefield, Reina is the quiet.

---

## Personality

Fierce. Direct. Protective to the point of instinct — her first response on seeing an unrecognised transformed entity in Scarlet's chambers was to draw and place herself between it and the room before she even knew it was Scarlet. That's not a decision. That's just who she is.

Reads situations fast once she has information. The shift from blind protective fury to tactical assessment when she understood the room's dynamics happened in seconds. She wouldn't have stopped Scarlet choosing violence against Vorlag. She silently urged caution anyway.

Utterly unshaken by Scarlet's transformation. Looked past the horns, the wings, the magma eyes, the obsidian skin, and found the person underneath immediately. Her response was not horror or awe. It was recognition, and then:

_"You look like you finally stopped asking for permission."_

---

## What She Knows

She calls Scarlet **Lyza**. The only one who does. The only one who knew her before any of this. What that name carries — what history lives in it — has not yet been fully explored.

She has been in love with Scarlet since childhood. This is not new information to her. Whether it is new information to Scarlet is a separate question.

---

## Current Status — Day 1 End State

Recovered from the east barracks by Aldric after being held by Commander Vorlag for "processing and assessment." Bruised but intact and furious. Now present in Scarlet's chambers in the western spire. Status within the castle formally unresolved — the treaty specified a personal guard, which she is, but her official standing has not been established beyond that.

Vorlag's fate is already sealed. She doesn't know the details. She'd approve.

## Relationships

- **Scarlet / Lyza** — the fixed point everything else orbits around. Has been since childhood. Not complicated in Reina's head even if it's complicated everywhere else.
- **Aldric** — retrieved her from the barracks without hesitation, greatsword already loose. She clocked his competence and his contempt for Vorlag. Respect established in a single hallway.
- **Lillinara** — first impression positive. "She looks at Lyza like she's the sunrise, not a monster." Reina noticed. Reina is perceptive.
- **The rest of the court** — unknown quantity. She's assessing.
