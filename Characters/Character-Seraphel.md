#character

**Seraphel** _The Mysterious Courtier. The Analyst. The One Who Keeps Score._

The demon king's elegant advisor and Lillinara's older sibling. Platinum blonde hair, violet eyes, androgynous features, immaculate composure. Presents male at court. The most consistently calculating presence in every room. Has been quietly recalibrating since the moment Scarlet arrived and hasn't stopped since.

---

## Appearance

Platinum blonde hair, usually tied back elegantly in courtier finery. Violet eyes that miss nothing. Androgynous, graceful, precise in every movement. Wears the role of elegant advisor like armour — it fits perfectly and reveals nothing.

Private study — discovered incidentally at the end of Day 1 — is the opposite of the austere strategy chamber aesthetic. Dresses, wigs, cosmetics organised with military precision. This has not been acknowledged by anyone yet.

---

## Nature

Analytical to the bone. Every interaction is data. Every variable gets logged. Scarlet describing the shape of her ambition in fifteen minutes on the battlements was filed away as efficiently as Lillinara stumbling on a smooth stone. Both are data points. Both matter.

Functions as Zephyrion's primary advisor and court manager. Understands the political architecture of the Night Realm better than almost anyone. Currently updating every model he has ever built because Scarlet exists.

Relationship to the Lady Sera identity unconfirmed in play but strongly implied by the wardrobe room and Lillinara's "brother's orders" framing. The secret the original scenario promises mindreading would reveal has not yet been directly surfaced. Scarlet knows about the wardrobe room. She hasn't said so.

Pronouns used as he/him in play so far. The full picture is unresolved and deliberately so.

---

## Personality

Cool. Precise. Occasionally cutting in a way that is entirely impersonal — he's not being unkind, he's being accurate. There's a difference and he's aware of it.

Fond exasperation is his primary mode when dealing with Lillinara. Protective of her in a quieter, more deliberate way than she is of him.

Has a sense of humour. It surfaces rarely and is dryer than the obsidian desert. "And to ensure you don't accidentally drain the life from the herb gardens." Said in the exact same tone as everything else.

Considers Scarlet's comfort a variable worth accounting for — unexpected. When she offered to curb flirting with Lillinara for his sake, he found it considerate in a way he hadn't anticipated. It changed the probability matrix.

Will absolutely tell you something is your idea when he's the one who made you think of it. Does this with elegant precision and zero remorse.
