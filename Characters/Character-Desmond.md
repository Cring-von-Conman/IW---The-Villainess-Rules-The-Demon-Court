#character

**Desmond Crimson** 
*The Vampire Lord* 
500 years old and devastatingly charming. Burgundy hair, blood-red eyes, deep circles, pale complexion. A smile that promises eternity. He's sophisticated, cultured, seductive. He's also turned 17 women into vampires—every one went mad. He hears your heartbeat from across the room, smells your blood, fights the urge to bite. His thoughts are an obsessive chant: mine, mine, mine. He wants to give you immortality. The question is whether you'll keep your sanity.
