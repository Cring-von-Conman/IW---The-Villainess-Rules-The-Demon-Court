#character

**Elara** _The Maid Who Stayed For Tea._

Young demon servant of the castle. Grey skin, wide eyes, perpetually nervous disposition that is slowly, cautiously thawing. Delivered the smoky quartz lenses on Seraphel's orders. Got invited to sit down and have honey cake. Changed her entire life trajectory in one evening.

---

## Appearance

Young. Grey skin. Wide eyes that take in everything and give away exactly what she's thinking. Moves with the careful economy of someone used to being invisible and slightly afraid of what happens when they're not.

---

## Nature

Castle servant. Before Scarlet's arrival her world was navigating the arbitrary cruelties of minor nobles, fetching for Lady Vex's spiteful daughter, and being blamed for broken trinkets. The prospect of being assigned to Scarlet directly felt monumental — safety, a clear chain of command, protection from casual cruelty.

Her thoughts are unguarded and honest. She doesn't have the political architecture for performance. What she thinks shows immediately on her face and in her mind. This is probably why Scarlet liked her.

---

## Personality

Nervous. Earnest. Has been trained into invisibility by years of castle service and is quietly startled every time she's treated as a person rather than furniture.

Perceptive underneath the nerves — she clocked Scarlet's nature accurately and immediately. "She's kind. She's strange and scary and her tears burn stone, but she's kind." That's a good read delivered fast. The nervousness is a surface condition, not a deep one.

Responds to genuine kindness with something close to relief rather than suspicion. The invitation to sit and have tea nearly undid her. "She asked. She actually asked. No one ever asks."

---

## What She's Done

- Delivered the smoky quartz lenses to Scarlet's chambers on Seraphel's urgent orders — first appearance
- Nearly overbalanced bowing on the way out
- Returned with Seraphel's message about Vorlag's situation
- Got invited to sit down for tea and honey cake
- Stayed
- Listened to the "what do you fancy" conversation with wide eyes and had a small quiet revelation that maybe being gay is just normal here
- Was guided out via servant passages by Lillinara, who is apparently now also her friend
- Accepted the position of permanent personal maid

---

## Current Status — Day 1 End State

Newly appointed personal maid to Lady Scarlet Ashcroft. Assignment not yet formally processed but verbally confirmed. Knows the servant passages to the western spire. Currently somewhere in the castle with Lillinara learning which ones they are.

Reina has already noted her as a useful point of access and has quietly slotted her into the security arrangements. She doesn't know this yet.

---

## Relationships

- **Scarlet** — went from "please don't let my hands shake" to "she's kind" to "I would be honoured" in one evening. Genuine loyalty forming fast. The kind that comes from being seen rather than being commanded.
- **Lillinara** — being shown the servant passages, probably being fed more cake. Immediate warmth on both sides.
- **Reina** — assessed as a useful ally and treated accordingly. Reina poured her tea and said "sit" in the same tone she uses with new recruits. Elara found this reassuring rather than alarming.
