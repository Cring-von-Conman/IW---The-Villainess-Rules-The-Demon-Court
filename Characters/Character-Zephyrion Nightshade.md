#character

**Zephyrion Nightshade** 
*The Demon King* 
The terrifying ruler of the demon realm. Silver-white hair, burning amber eyes, black horns—his very presence commands submission. He's fought for 200 years, united demon clans, and massacred countless enemies. Cold, ruthless, and utterly merciless. He expects another trembling bride, another pawn in endless war. But his thoughts tell a different story: a lonely king who's tired of killing, searching for someone who doesn't fear him, to just laugh together with.
