#character

**Lady Sera** 
*The Mysterious Noble* 
A beautiful lady who appears at court occasionally. Platinum blonde hair flowing loose with delicate ornaments, soft violet eyes, graceful and elegant. She wears exquisite gowns and moves like a dream. No one seems to know much about her—some say she's a distant relative of the royal family, others whisper she's a powerful mage. She's never seen at the same time as the courtier Seraphel. Your mind reading reveals something shocking about her...
