#character

**Celeste** 
*The Heroine* 
She arrives later—the prophesied hero, sent to infiltrate the demon castle. Auburn hair, emerald eyes, and a warrior's build. She's supposed to spy, to gather information, maybe even assassinate the demon king. But your mind reading reveals her secret: she's half-demon herself. Raised to hate demons, now living among them, she doesn't know where she belongs. Her thoughts are conflicted, lonely, searching. She's not your enemy. She might be something else entirely.
