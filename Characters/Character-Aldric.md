#character

**Aldric Stormbreaker** _The Fallen Paladin. The Grim Constant. The One Who Didn't Flinch._

Once the youngest Paladin Captain in human history. Now wears blackened armour and leads demon armies. Appeared three times in Day 1 without being summoned, said almost nothing each time, and every appearance landed. Has been assigned as Scarlet's flight trainer. Has not started yet. Already preparing for the probability that she does a barrel roll on her first attempt.

---

## Appearance

Massive. A scar crosses his face — a reminder of the day everything changed. Blackened armour that makes no sound when it shouldn't. Steel-gray eyes that assess everything with a soldier's cold economy. Radiates barely-controlled violence in the way that only something that has learned very precise control actually can.

---

## Nature

Fallen paladin. The guilt is real, constant, and deep — he remembers every death he caused. The self-loathing is structural rather than performative; it underpins everything but doesn't paralyse him. He converted it into function a long time ago.

Should hate Scarlet for being human. His thoughts say otherwise. The desperate need to protect one pure thing in his damned existence is present — but what Scarlet is becoming complicates the "pure" part in ways he hasn't fully processed yet. He approved of her transformation anyway. She chose it. That matters to him.

Appears and disappears almost at will. Not dramatic about it. Simply present when needed and absent when not. Scarlet gave him a long leash and he respected that immediately.

---

## Personality

Grim. Silent. Economy of words so extreme it reads as its own language. Everything he says lands because he says so little.

Responds to honesty with simple approval. The scream that caused a rockslide got "the scream was honest, that's better than a sword." That's high praise from Aldric.

Does not flinch. At anything. Scarlet's convergence, her transformation, her volcanic rage — all assessed with a soldier's eye and filed appropriately. The only flicker of something less controlled was "Vorlag" spoken as a single word dripping with contempt, and his immediate departure to retrieve Reina without waiting for permission.

Practical to the bone. "Living comes first. The paying back comes after. And it always costs more than you think." He knows the currency. He's paid it.

Has trained novice flyers before. The last one broke both legs. He expects Scarlet will probably just bounce. He is preparing for this.

## Relationships — Day 1 End State

- **Scarlet** — assigned protector and flight trainer. His protective instinct towards her is established but evolving — she is not the fragile thing he expected to protect and he is recalibrating what protection means in her context. Respects being read correctly. Respects the long leash. Has not spoken more than a handful of sentences to her directly and already feels substantial.
- **Reina** — retrieved her without hesitation. Clocked her competence immediately. Mutual respect established in a single hallway.
- **Zephyrion** — commands his armies. Long history. Assigned to Scarlet's training without question.
- **Vorlag** — contempt. Single word. The feeling is clearly not new.
