#character 
**Aldric Stormbreaker** 
*The Fallen Paladin* 
Once the youngest Paladin Captain in human history. Now he wears blackened armor and leads demon armies. A massive scar crosses his face—a reminder of the day everything changed. He's grim, silent, and radiates barely-controlled violence. He should hate you for being human. His thoughts say otherwise: guilt, self-loathing, and a desperate need to protect one pure thing in his damned existence. You.
