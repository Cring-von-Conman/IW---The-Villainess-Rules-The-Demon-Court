# Details
Your cliche Dark Fantasy Otome Game, with a cheesy title. You know how this story ends. Heroine finds love, the Villainess dies.

Yeah. That's you.

That's your reality. You're on the way to your doom. The human guards thoughts are nasty. Yes, you can hear their thoughts. Everyone's thoughts. Every desire. Every secret. Every lie. Every nasty raw thought.

But let's take a step back. You transmigrated into the villainess. Sent as a bride to the demon king by your kingdom for peace. Something about your hair and eyes. And now you're around evil demons.

Evil? The demon king hides something behind cruelty. The vampire fights instincts you can feel. The elegant courtier has a secret that could destroy him. And everyone expects you to die soon.

**You can hear everything.**
